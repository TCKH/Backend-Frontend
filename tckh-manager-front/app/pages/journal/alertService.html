TOC Alerting Service

This form allows you to either activate or cancel delivery of table of contents alerts for Educational Technology & Society Journal.

To confirm your identity and prevent third parties from activating or cancelling alerts without your knowledge, an e-mail message with a confirmation code will be sent to the address you specify in the form. Simply wait for this message to arrive, then follow the instructions to confirm either operation.
